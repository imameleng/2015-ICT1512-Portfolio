Some might think it tricky to make, but thats not the case.

The jars contain everything you need, including milk and dark chocolate and fresh almonds.

You have not lived untill you tasted freshly baked biscotti.

Method

Step one

Empty the contents of the jar into a large mixing bowl.

Step two

Add two eggs and 150 gram butter.

Step three

Mix thouroughly untill all the ingredients are combined.

Step four

Shape it all into a log and bake in the oven at 200 degrees for 35 minutes.

Step five

Remove from the oven and allow to cool for a couple of minutes. Cut into slices and place onto a baking tray.

Step six

Return to the oven and allow to dry for 10 minutes. Flip slices over and return to oven.

Step seven

Remove after drying and enjoy with coffee, tea or hot chocolate.
