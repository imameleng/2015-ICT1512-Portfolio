Deliciuos chocolate brownies made with rich cocoa and fresh pecan nuts.

Enjoy as a warm dessert with vanilla ice cream or share with unexpected guests.

The brownies takes about 40 minutes to prepare and fills the kitchen with the aroma of melted chocolate and freshly baked cake.

Method

Step one

Empty the contents of the jar into a large mixing bowl

Step two

Add two Eggs and 150 gram butter

Step three

Mix thoroughly until all the ingredients are combined

Step four

Bake in the oven at 180 degrees for 25 minutes, and enjoy
