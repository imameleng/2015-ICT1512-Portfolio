Freshly baked bread in only 5 easy steps, all you need is the jar and a 500ml buttermilk

Its a healthy alternative to yeast bread as no dry or wet yeast is added.

Wonderfully textured with muesli and sunflower seeds.

Method

Step one

Empty jar into a bowl.

Step two

Add the 500ml of buttermilk to mixture.

Step three

Mix untill all the ingredients are combined.

Step four

Transfer into greased bread pan and bake for ??? minutes.

Step five

Remove from oven and pan. Enjoy with butter, jam or cheese.
