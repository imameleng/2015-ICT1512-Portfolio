About Bake it Yourself

Bake it Yourself is a great example of a passion that became a hobby that became a business.

I started in 2012 when I was looking for Christmas gifts for the people at work, I could not find anything that was a little bit different, so I packaged some brownies and gave it to them. As soon as the rest of the staff saw it, they wanted some as well and thus I started selling them.


Three years down the line and B.i.Y is still going strong!

I did the Mosaiek Expo 2014 and the full range of products are currently available in Crafters market Clearwater and Cradlestone. I filled orders for Kenwood Home Appliances for their Celebrity Bake stars 2014 held in Cape Town and Johannesburg.
